A Proper Cornish Stay

More than a place to sleep

Tucked above one of Falmouth's most beloved locals, our rooms are a world away from the sterile anonymity of a hotel chain. Think thick wool blankets, creaking floorboards with character, the smell of woodsmoke drifting up from the bar below, and a view out over the rooftops towards the harbour.

Every stay includes a proper Cornish breakfast and a welcome drink at the bar. You are not just a guest — you are part of the local.
